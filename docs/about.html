About the product

Built for charity lending referrals, not automated rejection.

LendMatch uses LendingClub historical application data to estimate approval likelihood, default risk, and risk-based pricing. The app is designed to help caseworkers decide whether to refer, review, or support an applicant before a formal lender submission.

Models

Three scikit-learn gradient boosting models cover approval triage, default probability, and estimated APR. Metrics are published through the model card endpoint.

Controls

The interface adds validation, risk bands, partner matching, and caseworker guidance so results are understandable in a live charity presentation.

Limits

This is decision support. A real adoption should add consented credit checks, documented adverse-action rules, fairness monitoring, and legal review.
