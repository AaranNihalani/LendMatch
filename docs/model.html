Use

Intended workflow

LendMatch supports caseworkers by estimating referral readiness, risk band, likely pricing, and partner fit. It should be used before lender submission, alongside applicant consent and human review.

Controls

Deployment requirements

  • Document referral policy and human override rules.
  • Monitor outcomes by protected and vulnerability-related cohorts where legally appropriate.
  • Keep adverse-action, audit, and data-retention processes outside this prototype.

Limitations

Not a final credit decision
